Playoff Appearances/Hall Of Fame Players

Lakers Playoffs Appearances: 39 times (since 1971). Out of those 39 playoff appearance the Lakers have aquired 11 NBA championships, which is quite impressive. Now onto the H.O.F players (since 1971, Clippers birth). The Lakers have had H.O.F such as Steve Nash, Kareem Abdul-Jabbar, Shaquille O'Neal, Magic Johnson, James Worthy, Jamaal Wilkes, and Wilt Chamberlain.
